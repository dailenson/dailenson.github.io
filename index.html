About me

I'm a final-year Ph.D. student at South China University of Technology (SCUT), under the supervision of Prof. Shuangping Huang. Before my Ph.D., I was a master student in Software Engineering at SCUT, supervised by Associate Prof. Qin Du. Earlier, I received my B.Eng degree (2015-2019) from China University of Geosciences, beautifully located on the shores of East Lake in Wuhan.

I am excited to leverage deep generative techniques to synthesize personalized visual content, including image and vector generation.
